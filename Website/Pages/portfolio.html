GRAPHIC DESIGN

Paul Rand "Design and the Play Instinct"

The objective of this project is to lay out and design the book around a concept. My concept was the "l" in the play. Utilizing creative designs and artworks, I wanted to tell a simple, easy-to-read story that reflected the playful content. Besides the top row (cover and second page -with the "l" a cutout), each image is a spread (two pages).

Letterforms

This project requires the close examination of the form and function of letterforms. I investigated the unique visual characteristics of "Garamond" and "Fritz Quadrata" and the aspects of composition - including juxtaposition, contrast, form/counterform, symmetry, and balance. The two final products contrast yet balance each other. Garamond (left) blooms out like a flower, delicate yet grounded. Fritz (right) pull us in yet spins outwards, creating a unique dynamic pleasing to the viewerâ€™s eye.
